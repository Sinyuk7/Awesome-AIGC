# Control Poses

[Last Updated: 07/14/2023]

## ❓ Q - Can I control how my model poses? (i.e. look down, face the camera, look up, look to side)

 (i.e. model, posture, profile shot, look down, face the camera, look up, face away)

### There are portrait photography terms that may help:

- `profile shot`
- `three-quarter shot`
- `head-tilt pose`
- `hands-on-hips pose`
- `leaning pose`
- `S-curve pose`
- `crossed-arms pose`
- `hands-in-pockets pose`
- `sitting pose`
- `reclining pose`

If they don't work or are not what you  want, do both of these things together:

### 1️⃣ Give the model an emotion, attitude, or even an activity that is correlated to the position you want to see.

Here are example words:

- bashful, shy, embarrassed, awkward
- sleepy, relaxed, distracted
- intense, fierce, angry
- etc etc.

**AND** (do both!)

### 2️⃣ Add descriptive details of the face.

- bright eyes, intense gaze, fierce gaze
- straight nose, pronounced nose
- frowning, blushing, laughing
- symmetrical face, expressive face
