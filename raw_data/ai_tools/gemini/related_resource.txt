With Gems, you can create specific and repeatable instructions for Gemini to follow.

How Gems can be helpful
By creating a Gem with all of the info about your goals and preferences, or using a premade Gem designed for a certain action, the Gem is a shortcut every time you want to explore a topic with similar instructions. You can create different Gems for your unique needs and reuse them to simplify your prompts and questions in Gemini Apps.




Examples
With Gems, you can get responses that follow custom instructions like:

Workout requirements. Create a Gem that includes any physical limitations and time limits to make sure Gemini maximizes your time and provides suitable movements for each new workout.
Diet preferences. Create a Gem that has specific ingredients to include, or a diet to follow like vegan or high protein, every time you ask for a new recipe.
Gardening specifications. Create a Gem based on your garden's climate and light exposure to get relevant ideas whenever you ask Gemini gardening questions.

Gemini Apps comes with premade Gems to get started right away or customize for your needs.

On this page, learn how to:
	Chat with a premade Gem as is
	Customize a premade Gem

Chat with a premade Gem as is
Where you can chat with a premade Gem:
	The Gemini web app
	The Gemini mobile app
	The Gemini side panel in Google Workspace
Premade Gems offer help for a range of topics, from brainstorming and coding to career coaching.

Computer AndroidiPhone & iPad
To get started with a premade Gem:

On your computer, go to gemini.google.com.
On the left, click Explore Gems.
Under “Premade by Google,” select the Gem you want to try out.
Enter your prompt.
Click Submit .
Try it in the Gemini web app

Tip: Premade Gems already have instructions for the specific topic you select. When you provide a simple prompt, Gemini may ask follow up questions to further customize the response.

Customize a premade Gem
Where you can customize a premade Gem:
	The Gemini web app
You can copy some premade Gems as a starting point and customize it for your specific needs.

On your computer, go to gemini.google.com.
On the left, click Explore Gems.
Under “Premade by Google,” find the Gem you want to customize and click Make a copy .
Edit the Gem’s name.
Review and edit the Gem’s instructions to better fit your needs.
Click Save.
Try it in the Gemini web app


Examples
Copy the Career guide Gem and add details about your unique skills or career goals.
Copy the Writing editor Gem and add a specific writing style guide, like AP or MLA style, for the Gem to follow.
Copy the Brainstormer Gem and add info about your brainstorming goals, like an audience age range for story ideas or a decade for party theme ideas.
Tip: Not all premade Gems can be customized. If you don't have the "Make a copy" option, the Gem can't be copied or customized.
